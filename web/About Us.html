About Us


Helping Hands was founded in 2019 as a charitable organization dedicated to helping people suffering from crises, disasters and wars around the globe. We do that by joining forces with charities, national and international organization to deliver your donations to those who need them the most. Through us you have a way to donate to was victims and natural disaster sufferers. And you are sure people will receive them.
